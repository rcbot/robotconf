Robotconf Analyzer

What is this?

This page collects information about citation count, citation ranking of each paper of the major robotics conference (ICRA,IROS,RSS). Moreover, it calculates a quantification of impact of each conference by computing the number of papers with more than five citations.

Motivation

Being an ardent paper reader, I always wondered if I missed a paper or two in the last robotics conference. In our field, keeping track of what happens in the wide spectrum of robotics is pretty hard. Too many times I have asked myself if that related work section is updated with all the latest work. Do I really know all the high impact papers of that field?

Well, I decided to take matters into my own hands and tried to give an analytic answer to these questions.

Open data

Disclaimer

Even though I did my best for quality control, I am in no way responsible for the correctness of the results and the information gathered. This is neither an official project nor a professional product. I built it during my free time. My intentions are just to provide a useful tool to the community.

Data is parsed from official proceedings, citations from Google Scholar.

This page is automatically generated each six months. Last update: Jul 2014.
